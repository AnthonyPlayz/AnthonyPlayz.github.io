Easter Sunday

Easter Sunady is the day which the celebration of Jesus resurrection is celebrated

Christians celebrates the resurrection of Jesus Christ which occurred on the third day after he was crucified on the cross. 40 days prior to Easter, Christians follow Lent, which is supposed to be 40 days of prayer, penance and fasting.

Why Easter Sunday is important

The last week of this 40 day period is called Holy Week. During Holy Week, Holy Thursday commemorates when the Last Supper was held. Good Friday commemorates Jesus' crucifixion and death.
